A little about me

Hi there, my name is Sima. I’m a South African woman, who is currently living in the United States.

I’m a middle child of three, I have a big sister and a little brother. I’ve been traveling since I was 7 years old. My dad was South African, he experienced the unfortunate era of the apartheid regime. This lead him to be politically active while in college in pursuit of peace and harmony but when things got worse, during the time when Nelson Mandela was arrested my dad had to escaped South Africa to Britain avoiding being exiled. My mother was a Creole, born and bred in Réunion. My culture was heavily influenced by Creole and South African traditional values.
